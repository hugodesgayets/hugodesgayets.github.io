Mapstr lets users saves every places in the world on their own personal map.

is an app that let you save every places in the world and share your map with your firends. Rated 5 stars on the Appstore, we raised 700m euros

When I stared, Mapstr was in its early, close to the intial MVP. I was solely in charge of both UI and UX of mapstr.

I worked on X projects Working on both sides was challenging because I had to adapt a very dualistic worklow: Make UX project get UI implement easily and vice versa. I was in a charge of building a team process.

Here the things I’ve learned and put in place as a UX designer:
- User research (data vizualisation / user sessions / reporting)
- Internal tools (emails templates / analytics)
- Documentation (visual styleguide, sitemaps, user flows)
- Prototyping

Unified system of interface guidelines
